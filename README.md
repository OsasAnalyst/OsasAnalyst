## üåü Flagship Project: Store Item Demand Forecasting (Time Series)

My top project demonstrates mastery of predictive time series modeling, crucial for retail inventory optimization.

<div style="background-color: #151515; padding: 25px; border-radius: 10px; border: 2px solid #0E75B6;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0; color: #fff; font-size: 1.8em;">Store Item Demand Forecasting: Reducing Stockouts and Capital Waste</h3>
    
    <p style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #ccc;">
        <b>Objective:</b> To move e-commerce inventory management beyond simple moving averages by building **robust, data-driven forecasting models** capable of minimizing stockouts (lost sales) and overstocking (wasted storage).
    </p>

    <p style="font-weight: bold; color: #0E75B6;">üõ†Ô∏è Technical Approach & Impact:</p>
    <ul style="list-style-type: square; padding-left: 20px; line-height: 1.6; color: #ccc;">
        <li>Modeled and compared three families: Classical (**ARIMA/SARIMA**), **Machine Learning** (Random Forest, **LightGBM/XGBoost**), and **Deep Learning** (LSTM/GRU).</li>
        <li>Identified **tree-based ensemble models** as the most consistent performers, providing significant accuracy gains over naive baselines.</li>
        <li>Achieved accuracy gains that directly support **better inventory planning** and **higher customer satisfaction** at scale.</li>
    </ul>
